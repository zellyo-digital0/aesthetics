Meet Karolina Jenras

Karolina Jendras is a leading Registered Independent Nurse Prescriber (INP) and advanced Aesthetic Injector with a progressive approach to medical-grade aesthetics.

Following her graduation from Kings College in 2012 with a BSc in Adult Nursing the KJ Aesthetic was honed during her time working for and being guided and influenced by, one of the most respected Cosmetic Surgeons practicing advanced aesthetic techniques and facial rejuvenation procedures in the UK.

Being appointed by a new-generation, surgical luminary immediately after graduation afforded Karolina rare and invaluable insight for such a young professional into the detail, care, focus, and diligence only a surgical environment can provide. Here the bedrock of Karolina’s meticulous and considered style, of which Karolina is widely known, was being firmly cultivated. 

Five years on, and after obtaining her Nurse Prescriber Certification, Karolina established herself at one of London’s principal private clinics where she built on her Aesthetic Nurse training. It’s here, continuing to be guided by international leaders in the advanced aesthetics field that Karolina started to garner her reputation and an ardent clientele seeking her crafted and considered facial rejuvenation style. It was time for Karolina to launch her own practice.

Karolina’s “less is more” approach to injectable treatments may sound simple, but it is anything but that. Everything from advancements in facial aesthetics, the client's personal goals, and most importantly - the facial structure, proportions, and unique features are meticulously studied by Karolina before any treatment is provided.  Subtlety, softness, and the enhancement of natural beauty are the hallmarks of Karolina’s exceptional work, and the demand for her much-sought-after style continues to flourish.
